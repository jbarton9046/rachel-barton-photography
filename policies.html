Policies & Model Release

Please review before booking. By scheduling online, you agree to the terms below.

Booking & Payment Reschedule & Weather Model Release Editing & Delivery Usage & Printing Prep & Arrival

Booking & Payment

  • Retainer: A non-refundable $150 retainer is due at booking to hold your time. The balance is due 1 day before the session.
  • Payment methods: Online card payments are accepted via our booking page.
  • Sales tax/fees: If applicable, taxes or processing fees are shown at checkout.

Rescheduling, Weather & Cancellation

  • Reschedule: One complimentary reschedule with 24+ hours’ notice, subject to availability.
  • Weather: We monitor forecasts. If conditions are unsafe or not photo-friendly, we’ll choose a backup date/time or a nearby covered location.
  • No-show/late: Arrivals 15+ minutes late reduce shooting time. No-shows forfeit the retainer.

Model Release

You grant Rachel Barton Photography permission to use images from your session for portfolio, website, social media, and marketing. If you prefer privacy, let us know before your session and we’ll note a no-share preference.

Editing, Turnaround & Delivery

  • Editing style: Candid, true-to-life color with natural skin tones. Light retouching at our discretion.
  • Delivery: Private online gallery with high-resolution downloads and optional print shop.
  • Turnaround: Typical delivery within 2–3 weeks. Rush options may be available on request.

Client Usage & Printing

  • Personal license: You may share, post, and print for personal use. Please credit when posting online.
  • Edits: Please don’t apply filters or alter images beyond cropping for social media.
  • Commercial use: Requires written permission and a separate license.

Prep & Arrival

  • Neutral/coastal tones photograph beautifully; coordinate without matching.
  • Bring water, wipes, a small snack for little ones, and a change of clothes if desired.
  • Golden hour (sunrise/sunset) is recommended for beach sessions.
