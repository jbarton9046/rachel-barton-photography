Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about booking, sessions, styling, weather plans, delivery, and more.

Q How do I book?

Use the Book button to open my live calendar. If you don’t see a time that works, email me and I’ll try to accommodate.

Q What areas do you serve?

Sarasota • Siesta Key • Lido • Longboat Key • Lakewood Ranch • Venice • Bradenton • Tampa • St. Petersburg • Clearwater • Naples.

Q What’s included in a session?
  • Pre-session planning (location, styling tips, timing)
  • On-location session (typically 45–75 minutes)
  • Hand-edited gallery with high-resolution downloads
  • Personal print release
Q Do you photograph extended families?

Yes! For groups larger than a single household, we’ll plan extra time to capture combinations. Tell me your headcount when booking.

Q What if it rains or is very windy?

We’ll watch the forecast and decide together. I hold backup slots for weather reschedules—no fee to move due to unsafe or unflattering conditions.

Q What should we wear?

Neutral, soft tones photograph beautifully on the Gulf Coast. I’ll send a simple styling guide with palettes and do-and-don’ts once you book.

Q When will we receive our photos?

Galleries are typically delivered within 2–3 weeks. I always send a few sneak peeks within a couple of days when possible.

Q How are images delivered?

Via an online gallery—download in high-resolution and web-size. You can also order professional prints right from the gallery.

Q Do you work with newborns?

Yes—natural, in-home lifestyle sessions focused on connection over posing. Ideal timing is within the first 2–6 weeks, but truly lovely anytime.

Q Can we bring pets?

Absolutely! Please let me know in advance so we can choose a pet-friendly location and plan for a helper if needed.

Q What’s your payment and cancellation policy?

A retainer is due at booking to hold your date; the balance is due by session day. If you need to reschedule, just reach out—life happens!

Q Any travel fees?

No travel fee within the local Sarasota/Manatee area. Small fees may apply for farther beaches or parks—I'll confirm before booking.

Q Accessibility & comfort

If stairs, sand, or walking distances are a concern, we’ll choose an easy-access spot. Tell me what you need and I’ll handle the details.
