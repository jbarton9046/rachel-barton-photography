Meet Rachel

Mom of two, outdoors lover, and detail-driven photographer who keeps sessions calm, playful, and effortless.

Two years ago, our family moved from Nashville to Sarasota, and we’ve been chasing beach sunrises and golden-hour palms ever since. Before photography, I worked in corporate healthcare administration and earned an MBA in Healthcare Management — so I bring a calm, organized, detail-focused approach to every session.

I’m known for gentle direction that still feels candid and natural, true-to-life color, and a kid-friendly flow with room to wiggle and wonder. I shoot across Sarasota, Siesta Key, Lido, Longboat, Lakewood Ranch, and Venice — and I’m happy to travel from Tampa down to Naples.

Favorite locations: Lido Key, Siesta Key, Celery Fields, Myakka River State Park, The Ringling, Selby Gardens.
