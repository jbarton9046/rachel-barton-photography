What should we wear?

Neutral + coastal tones photograph beautifully. Avoid tiny stripes/logos; coordinate without matching. I’ll share simple styling tips after booking.

How far in advance should we book?

2–4 weeks is ideal for weekday evenings; weekends fill sooner. If you have a tight window, reach out—cancellations pop up.

What happens if it rains?

We’ll watch the forecast together. I hold backup times for weather and can pivot to a nearby covered location when needed.
