Meet Rachel

Mom of two, outdoors lover, and detail-driven photographer who keeps sessions calm, playful, and effortless.

Two years ago, our family moved from Nashville to Sarasota, and we’ve been chasing beach sunrises and golden-hour palms ever since. Before photography, I worked in corporate healthcare administration and earned an MBA in Healthcare Management — so I bring a calm, organized, detail-focused approach to every session.

I’m known for gentle direction that still feels candid and natural, true-to-life color, and a kid-friendly flow with room to wiggle and wonder. I shoot across Sarasota, Siesta Key, Lido, Longboat, Lakewood Ranch, and Venice — and I’m happy to travel from Tampa down to Punta Gorda.

Favorite locations: Lido Key, Siesta Key, Celery Fields, Myakka River State Park, The Ringling, Selby Gardens.

FAQ & Policies

Availability

Weekday early mornings & evenings; full weekends. Golden hour recommended for beach sessions.

Booking & Payment

A signed agreement and a non-refundable retainer (e.g., $100 or 25%) secure your date; balance due by the session day.

Rescheduling & Weather

Florida skies change fast. If rain or high winds are likely, we’ll decide together 12–24h prior and reschedule at no fee once.

Turnaround

Proofs within 7–10 days; full gallery within 2–4 weeks. Rush editing available for an additional fee.

Delivery & Prints

Online gallery with high-resolution downloads and print options. Personal print rights included.

Travel

Core area: Sarasota, Siesta Key, Lido, Longboat, Lakewood Ranch, Venice. Travel beyond may include a small fee.
